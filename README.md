## About The Project

[![Product Name Screen Shot][product-screenshot]](https://example.com)

There are many great Password Managers out there, but I didn't want to put my trust in some online manager that would store my sensitive information in the cloud. LockBox aims to provide the user with full control of the safety of their data by storing passwords locally.

Here's why:
* **You shouldn't keep sensitive data in the hands of someone else:** Entrusting your passwords and sensitive information to a third-party cloud service means you're reliant on their security measures. By storing data locally, you maintain full control over who has access to your information and can better safeguard your privacy.

* **Local storage reduces dependency on internet access:** With LockBox, your passwords are always accessible, even without an internet connection. This ensures you can retrieve your important information anytime, anywhere, without relying on external servers or connectivity.

* **Enhanced data protection:** Storing your passwords locally allows you to implement your own security measures. You can encrypt your data using your preferred methods, providing an additional layer of security tailored to your needs.
